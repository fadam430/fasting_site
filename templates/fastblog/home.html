What is fasting?

Fasting is the voluntary abstention from some or all food and drink for a set period. It can be used for health, spiritual, or medical reasons. Common approaches include time-restricted eating and multi-day fasts.

Why we use it?

People fast for many reasons: weight management, improved metabolic health, mental clarity, or spiritual practice. Scientific studies show benefits for insulin sensitivity and cellular maintenance when practiced safely.
